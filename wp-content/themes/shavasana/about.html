Angela Logan

Angela Logan has practiced yoga for over 10 years, and has taught yoga since 2008. She received the majority of her training, including Hot Yoga, at Y+ Yoga Center, Shanghai, China. Prior to returning to the U.S. after serving a 10 year expatriate work assignment with her husband and family, all of her teaching experience was based in Asia. Now residing in the Columbia River Gorge, she is very enthusiastic and motivated to have the opportunity to share her international experience with fellow practitioners here in the Hood River area.
